About Us

Corndoglicious is a small business that offers corndogs made from hotdogs cut into four pieces, coated in corn batter, and served with ketchup, mayonnaise, or other flavor options.

Our goal is to provide an affordable and convenient snack that is suitable for quick meals, casual gatherings, or on-the-go eating.

Our corndogs are supplied by a family-operated business located at 20 J. Ferrer Street, Guerrero Compound, San Isidro, Parañaque City, Metro Manila, ensuring consistency in preparation and supply.
